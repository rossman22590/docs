Follow these steps to create your custom song:

## Mastering SongCraft Prompts

Crafting effective prompts and lyrics is crucial for achieving the best results with SongCraft. Here are some tips and techniques:

### Verse and Chorus Structure

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Basic Structure" icon="layer-group">
    A typical structure includes a verse and chorus in each clip:
    ```
    [Verse]
    (Lyrics for verse)
    
    [Chorus]
    (Lyrics for chorus)
    ```
    - Verses are usually rhythmic and restrained
    - Choruses have more melody and energy, acting as the song's 'hook'
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Using Tags" icon="tags">
    Enhance your lyrics with descriptive tags:
    ```
    [Sad Verse]
    [Happy Chorus]
    [Rapped Verse]
    [Powerpop Chorus]
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Advanced Song Elements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pre-chorus and Bridge" icon="text">
    Use these elements for transitions and breaks in pattern:
    ```
    [Pre-Chorus]
    (Transition lyrics)
    
    [Bridge]
    (Break from main pattern)
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Other Song Parts" icon="music">
    Incorporate various song elements:
    - [Intro]
    - [Hook]
    - [Break] or [Percussion Break]
    - [Interlude]
    - [Outro] or [Refrain]
    - [Big Finish]
    - [End]
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Instrumental and Voice Tags

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Instrumental Sections" icon="guitar">
    Guide instrumental parts with tags like:
    - [Melodic Bass]
    - [Fingerstyle Guitar Solo]
    - [Bass Drop]
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Voice Styles" icon="microphone">
    Influence the singing voice:
    - Describe in style prompt: "sultry male singer", "female narrator"
    - Use tags: [Female Narrator], [Diva Solo], [Gospel Choir]
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with SongCraft, try these solutions:

1. **Unclear Style**: Be more specific in your style description and tags.
2. **Poor Lyrics**: Try generating new lyrics or refine your input for better results.
3. **Inconsistent Sound**: Ensure your style tags align with your chosen genre and theme.
4. **Generation Errors**: Refresh and try again, or slightly modify your inputs.
