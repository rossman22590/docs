# TempoCraft: AI Music Generation

<Tip>
  Unleash your creativity with TempoCraft! Generate songs with lyrics or instrumentals, powered by Tempolar.
</Tip>

## Overview

TempoCraft, powered by Tempolar, is a cutting-edge music generation tool that allows you to create songs with lyrics or instrumentals. With features like AI-powered search and voice generation, TempoCraft is built to streamline your workflow, letting you create, edit, and innovate with ease.

## How to Generate a Song with Lyrics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Song">
    Describe the song you want to make first to generate lyrics and structure with AI, or paste in your own lyrics and use the lyrics builder to adjust the structure of the lyrics.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft1.png" alt="Describe Your Song" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Title and Model">
    The title will be automatically generated. Adjust it and the model if necessary.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft2.png" alt="Adjust Title and Model" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Voice (Optional)">
    Optionally select a voice for your performer. You can also clone a voice by uploading audio.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft3.png" alt="Select a Voice" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a MIDI File (Optional)">
    You can also optionally select a MIDI file to have even more control over your song's instrumentals.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft4.png" alt="Select a MIDI File" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Song">
    Click "Generate Song" and wait for the result. It will return the audio as well as stems.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft5.png" alt="Generate Song" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Generate an Instrumental

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Instrumental">
    Select "Instrumental" on top.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft9.png" alt="Select Instrumental" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Instrumental">
    Describe the kind of instrumental that you would like. Add a title for your song as well.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft10.png" alt="Describe Your Instrumental" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Instrumental Configuration">
    Select the instrumental configuration for your song, including Tonality, BPM, and chords.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft11.png" alt="Select Instrumental Configuration" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Instrumental">
    Click "Generate Instrumental".
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft12.png" alt="Generate Instrumental" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Building Lyrics

Once lyrics are generated you can open the lyrics builder.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Lyrics Builder">
    Once lyrics are generated you can open the lyrics buider.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft6.png" alt="Open Lyrics Builder" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Structure Your Lyrics">
    Each section of the lyrics is seperated by the name of that section in brackets, this lets the AI know to adjust instrumental and where it is in the song. For example [Chorus], [Bridge], and etc
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft7.png" alt="Structure Your Lyrics" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Lyrics">
    Dont forget to click save lyrics otherwise your changes will not be applied.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/tempocraft8.png" alt="Save Your Lyrics" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
