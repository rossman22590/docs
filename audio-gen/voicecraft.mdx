# VoiceCraft: AI-Powered Voice Creation and Conversion

<Tip>
  Unlock the power of AI to create unique voices, clone existing ones, and convert audio with VoiceCraft!
</Tip>

## Overview

VoiceCraft, powered by Eleven Labs, is a versatile AI tool that provides a suite of functionalities for working with voices. It allows you to design entirely new AI voices from a description, clone voices from audio samples, and convert existing speech using a specified voice ID. Whether you're a content creator, developer, or just exploring AI audio, VoiceCraft offers powerful tools for voice manipulation.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Voice Creator" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Design **custom AI voices** from a text description and sample text.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Speech to Speech" icon="rotate">
    Convert an **uploaded audio file** to a different voice using a voice ID.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clone Voice" icon="user-plus">
    Create a **clone of a voice** from uploaded audio samples.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Text to Dialogue" icon="comments">

  </Card>

## Voice Creator: Design Custom Voices

This feature allows you to generate new AI voices based on a descriptive text and a sample text for the voice to speak.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/voicecraft-voicecreator.png" alt="VoiceCraft Voice Creator Screenshot" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe the Voice" icon="pencil">
    In the **Voice Description** field, enter a detailed description of the voice you want to create (e.g., "A warm, friendly female voice with a slight British accent"). A minimum length is required for the description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Text to Speak" icon="keyboard">
    In the **Text to Speak** field, enter the sample text you want the generated voice previews to say. The text must be between 100 and 1000 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Voice Previews" icon="play">
    Click the **Generate Voice Previews** button. The AI will generate several audio previews of potential voices based on your description and text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Preview" icon="list-check">
    Listen to the generated previews. For the voice you like, click the **Select** button next to its audio player. This will populate the **Generated Voice ID** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Voice" icon="text">
    In the **Voice Name** field, enter a name for the voice you are creating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Voice" icon="circle-check">
    Click the **Create Voice** button. This will finalize the creation of your custom voice, and you will receive its unique Voice ID. The voice will also be saved to your Voice History.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Voice Creator Input Parameters:

<ResponseField name="Voice Description" type="string" required>
  A text description of the voice characteristics (minimum length required).
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="Text to Speak" type="string" required>
  Sample text for voice previews (100-1000 characters).
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="Voice Name" type="string" required>
  A name for the custom voice you are creating.
</ResponseField>

## Speech to Speech: Convert Audio Voice

This feature allows you to change the voice of an existing audio file to a different voice using its Voice ID.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/voicecraft-speechtospeech.png" alt="VoiceCraft Speech to Speech Screenshot" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Audio File" icon="upload">
    Click **Choose File** to upload the audio file you want to convert. The audio must be 60 seconds or less.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Voice ID" icon="key">
    In the **Voice ID** field, enter the ID of the voice you want to convert the audio to. You can get Voice IDs from the Voice Creator, Clone Voice, or your Voice History.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Convert Speech" icon="play">
    Click the **Convert Speech** button. The AI will process your uploaded audio and convert the speech to the specified voice. Note that this process costs credits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Speech to Speech Input Parameters:

<ResponseField name="Upload Audio File" type="file" required>
  The audio file to convert (max 60 seconds).
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="Voice ID" type="string" required>
  The ID of the target voice for conversion.
</ResponseField>

### Speech to Speech Credits:

Converting speech using this feature costs **1 credit per second** of the input audio.

## Clone Voice: Create a Voice Clone

This feature allows you to create a new AI voice by cloning it from one or more uploaded audio samples of the voice you want to replicate.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/voicecraft-voicecloner.png" alt="VoiceCraft Clone Voice Screenshot" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Name Your Voice" icon="text">
    In the **Voice Name** field, enter a name for the voice clone you are creating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the Voice (Optional)" icon="pencil">
    In the **Description** field, you can optionally provide a description of the voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Audio Files" icon="upload">
    Click the **Select Audio Files** button to upload one or more audio samples of the voice you want to clone. Higher quality and longer samples generally yield better results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Background Noise (Optional)" icon="sliders">
    Check the **Remove Background Noise** box if you want the AI to attempt to clean up the audio samples during the cloning process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clone Voice" icon="user-plus">
    Click the **Clone Voice** button. The AI will process your audio samples and create a new Voice ID for the cloned voice. This process costs credits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Clone Voice Input Parameters:

<ResponseField name="Voice Name" type="string" required>
  A name for the voice clone.
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="Description" type="string">
  (Optional) A description of the voice.
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="Audio Files" type="array of files" required>
  One or more audio samples of the voice to clone.
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="Remove Background Noise" type="boolean">
  Toggle to enable background noise removal during cloning.
</ResponseField>

### Clone Voice Credits:

Creating a voice clone costs credits based on the total length of the uploaded audio samples. The cost is **1 credit per second** of audio.

## Text to Dialogue: Create a Conversation

This feature allows you to create a dialogue between multiple speakers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Voice">
    Select a voice. It can either be one of the voices from Eleven Labs library or a voice you have created using either Voice Designer of Voice Clone.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/voicecraft1.png" alt="Select a Voice" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Your Dialogue">
    Start writing your dialogue, a set of lines for each speaker to say and go back and forth.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/voicecraft2.png" alt="Write Your Dialogue" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Speakers">
    To continue after two blocks of dialogue, click add speaker. Now here you can continue with one of the speakers we are already using or introduce a new speaker. This can safely support up to 8 speakers. Make sure to select the voice. If you are continuing with a speaker you already used, then make sure to select the right voice and have the same name.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/voicecraft3.png" alt="Add Speakers" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Dialogue">
    Once you are done with the plan and the script, Click Generate Dialogue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## History

VoiceCraft keeps a history of your created voices and speech conversions.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="View Voice History" icon="list">
    Click the **View Voice History** button (available in Voice Creator and Clone Voice modes) to see a list of voices you have created or cloned, including their names and Voice IDs. From here, you can select a voice to use in Speech to Speech or Text to Speech.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="View Speech History" icon="clock">
    Click the **View Speech History** button (available in Clone Voice mode) to see a history of audio files you have generated using your cloned voices.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Conversion History" icon="clock">
    (Available in Speech to Speech mode) See a history of audio files you have converted using the Speech to Speech converter.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Clear History" icon="trash">
    Each history section has a **Clear History** button to remove all entries from that specific history list.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Credits

Your total credit balance is displayed at the top of the interface. Generating voice previews (Voice Creator), converting speech (Speech to Speech), and cloning voices (Clone Voice) all consume credits.

<Tip>
  Click the **Buy More** button to purchase additional credits if needed.
</Tip>

## Tips for Best Results

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Detailed Voice Description (Voice Creator)" icon="pencil">
    Provide a rich and specific description of the voice you want to create for better preview generation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="High-Quality Audio Samples (Clone Voice)" icon="volume-high">
    Use clear, high-fidelity audio samples with minimal background noise for the best voice cloning results.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accurate Voice ID (Speech to Speech)" icon="key">
    Ensure the Voice ID you enter is correct for the target voice you want to convert to.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage Audio Lengths" icon="clock">
    Be mindful of the maximum audio lengths for uploads (60s for Speech to Speech, no strict limit specified for Clone Voice but shorter, clean samples are better) and credit consumption.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

VoiceCraft offers a comprehensive suite of tools for AI voice manipulation. Whether you're creating a new voice from scratch, cloning an existing one, or converting audio, its intuitive interface and powerful AI models make advanced voice work accessible.
