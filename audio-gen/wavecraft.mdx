# WaveCraft: AI-Powered Audio Creation Suite

<Tip>
  Generate custom instrumental tracks, unique sound effects, or add sound to your videos with WaveCraft's versatile AI!
</Tip>

## Overview

WaveCraft is a comprehensive AI tool designed for audio generation and manipulation. It offers three distinct modes: **Instrumental**, **Sound Effect**, and **Video Sound**, allowing you to create various types of audio content from text descriptions or videos. Whether you need background music, a specific sound effect, or want to enhance a video's audio, WaveCraft provides the tools to bring your auditory ideas to life.

## How to Use WaveCraft

WaveCraft features three modes, accessible via the tabs at the top of the interface.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Mode" icon="list-ul">
    Choose the mode that matches your goal: **Instrumental**, **Sound Effect**, or **Video Sound**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Input" icon="keyboard">
    - For **Instrumental** or **Sound Effect**: Enter a text description of the audio you want to generate in the **Music/Sound Effect Description** field.
    - For **Video Sound**: Upload or drag and drop a video file into the designated area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Duration (Instrumental/Sound Effect)" icon="clock">
    If in Instrumental or Sound Effect mode, use the slider to set the desired **Duration** for the generated audio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Audio" icon="play">
    Click the corresponding generate button: **Generate Music**, **Generate Sound Effect**, or **Add Sound to Video**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Input Parameters and Options

WaveCraft offers different inputs based on the selected mode:

### Common Parameters:

<ResponseField name="Music/Sound Effect Description" type="string" required>
  (Instrumental & Sound Effect Modes) A text description guiding the AI on the desired audio content or style.
</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="Duration" type="integer">
  (Instrumental & Sound Effect Modes) Sets the length of the generated audio clip in seconds.
  - Instrumental: 1s to 180s
  - Sound Effect: 1s to 30s
</ResponseField>

### Video Sound Mode Specific:

<ResponseField name="Source Video" type="file">
  Upload a video file (MP4 recommended) to provide visual context for audio generation. Maximum 30 seconds.
</ResponseField>

## My Creations / History

WaveCraft keeps a history of all your generated audio creations.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="View Creations" icon="list">
    Your generated audios will appear in the **My Creations** section below the main interface.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Latest Creation" icon="star">
    The most recently generated audio will also be displayed in the **Latest Creation** panel for quick access and playback.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Playback" icon="play">
    Use the built-in audio players in both the Latest Creation and My Creations sections to listen to your generated audio.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Download" icon="download">
    Download individual audio files (WAV for Instrumental/Sound Effect, MP4 for Video Sound).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Download All" icon="arrow-down-to-bracket">
    Download all creations in your history as a ZIP file.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Clear All" icon="trash">
    Clear all generated audios from your history.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with WaveCraft, consider these solutions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Audio Doesn't Match Description" icon="comment-slash">
    Refine your text prompt to be more specific. Try different phrasing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Video Sound Doesn't Match Video" icon="video-slash">
    Ensure the visual content of your video is clear and strongly suggests the desired audio. Try a different video.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Processing Errors" icon="bug">
    Check your internet connection. If uploading a video, ensure it's in a supported format (MP4) and within the 30-second limit.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
