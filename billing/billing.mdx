# Billing

When you subscribe to **AI Tutor** or **Pixio**, you may notice that your billing statement displays **AI TUTOR APP** as the transaction label. This is a shared billing identifier for both services, ensuring a streamlined and efficient payment process.

## Why Does It Say AI TUTOR APP?

Both **AI Tutor** and **Pixio** are part of the same ecosystem and use a unified payment processor. To keep things simple, we use the label **AI TUTOR APP** for all transactions associated with these products.

## Example Statement

Here's how the charge might appear on your credit card statement:

AI TUTOR APP - $XX.XX

The amount charged will correspond to the subscription tier or services you've selected for either **AI Tutor** or **Pixio**.
