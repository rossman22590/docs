## How to Get Support

### 1. Submit a Support Request

The best way to get support is by submitting a support request through our official channels. Please include the following information to help us resolve your issue quickly:

- A detailed description of the issue.
- Steps to reproduce the problem (if applicable).
- Screenshots or error messages (if available).
- Your contact information.

### 2. Response Time

Our support team aims to respond to all requests within **24-72 hours**.\
We appreciate your patience as we work to address your concerns.
