This user-friendly tool makes it easy to revitalize your cherished memories, bringing the past into the present with fresh, realistic colors.

#### How to Use Colorize

Follow these simple steps to transform your black-and-white photos into colorful memories.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Select Your File" icon="file-image">
    Click the **'File' button** to open a window where you can browse and select the photo you want to colorize.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Choose Your Model" icon="paintbrush">
    Select either the **Artistic Model** for vibrant, creative colors, or the **Stable Model** for more natural, realistic tones.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Adjust Render Factor" icon="sliders">
    Use the **'Render Factor' setting** to control the intensity of colors. Lower for subtle tones, higher for bold colors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Colorize Your Image" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Click the **'Colorize Image' button** to start the colorization process. The AI will analyze and apply colors to your photo.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Download Your Colorized Photo" icon="download">
    Once colorization is complete, click the **'Download' button** to save your newly colorized photo to your device.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Choose the Right Model" icon="paintbrush">

  <Accordion title="Experiment with Render Factor" icon="sliders">
