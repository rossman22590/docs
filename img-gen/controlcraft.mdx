### ControlCraft on Pixio combines standard AI models with innovative ControlNet technology, offering precise control over subject-specific modifications within images.

This advanced tool caters to users seeking nuanced editing capabilities for detailed project work, professional photo editing, or creative explorations requiring targeted adjustments.

#### How to Use ControlCraft

Follow these steps to harness the power of ControlCraft for advanced image editing.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Image Upload or Input" icon="upload">
    Begin by **uploading the image** you wish to edit or entering its URL directly into ControlCraft.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Choose Your Models" icon="cubes">
