### CustomCraft on Pixio offers unparalleled customization in AI-generated images, allowing users to input specific models and LoRAs for highly tailored results.

Designed for professionals and advanced users, CustomCraft provides the precision and flexibility needed for projects that demand unique or specialized model capabilities, empowering creators to achieve their exact vision.

#### Key Features of CustomCraft
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Custom Model Selection" icon="cube">
    Input specific **Civit URLs** or **Hugging Face IDs** to access a wide range of AI models tailored to different styles, subjects, or technical requirements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Detailed Prompting" icon="pen-fancy">
    Utilize both **positive and negative prompts** to guide the AI with precision, specifying what to include and what to avoid in the generated images.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Comprehensive Customization" icon="sliders">
    Adjust settings such as **image size**, **format**, **model architecture**, and optional **seed** for replicating results.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Post-Generation Options" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    After generation, choose to **download**, **upscale**, or **animate** your images for further refinement.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

#### How to Use CustomCraft

Follow these steps to harness the full power of CustomCraft and create highly specialized AI-generated images.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Custom Model Input" icon="cube">
    Begin by entering the **Civit URL** or **Hugging Face ID** of your chosen AI model. Find this URL by clicking the download button next to the desired model or LoRA on Civit, then copy and paste the link address into CustomCraft.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Input Prompts and Customize" icon="pen-to-square">
    Enter detailed **positive and negative prompts** to direct the AI's output. Be specific about what you want to include and exclude in your image.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Fine-Tune Settings" icon="sliders">
    Utilize the advanced settings to adjust the **number of inference steps**, **guidance scale**, **clip skip**, and decide on the **number of images** you wish to generate. Refer to Civit for recommended settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Generate and Refine" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    With all preferences set, proceed to generate your images. After generation, choose to **download**, **upscale**, or **animate** your creations for further refinement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Tips for Success with CustomCraft

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Select Models Wisely" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Choose models that are **well-suited to your project's style and content requirements** for optimal results. Consider the strengths of different models for your specific needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Utilize Both Prompts Effectively" icon="pen-fancy">
    Leverage both **positive and negative prompts** to significantly refine the AI's focus, leading to more accurate and satisfying outputs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Experiment with Advanced Controls" icon="flask">
    The advanced settings offer powerful tools to influence the generation process. **Experiment with different configurations** to achieve the desired balance between creativity and precision.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Document Settings for Consistency" icon="clipboard">
    When you achieve desired results, **document the specific settings and model IDs** used. This practice ensures the ability to replicate success in future projects.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
