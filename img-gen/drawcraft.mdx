### DrawCraft on Pixio merges traditional drawing techniques with cutting-edge AI technology, offering a versatile platform for creative expression and image manipulation.

This innovative tool allows users to draw, input images, and customize their creations with AI-guided enhancements, color adjustments, and even animation options.

#### Key Features of DrawCraft
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AI-Guided Creation" icon="robot">
    Input **descriptive prompts** to guide the AI in enhancing and transforming your drawings or uploaded images.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flexible Drawing Tools" icon="pencil">
    Utilize a **comprehensive toolbar** for creating boxes, selecting areas, and detailed customization of your artwork.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Color Enhancement" icon="palette">
    Use the **DrawFast icon** to add and adjust colors, bringing vibrancy to your creations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Animation Options" icon="film">
    Explore the ability to **animate your creations**, adding an exciting dynamic dimension to your work.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

#### How to Use DrawCraft

Follow these steps to harness the full power of DrawCraft for your creative projects.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Utilize the Toolbar" icon="hammer">
    Locate the toolbar at the bottom to **create boxes** or **select specific areas** of your image for detailed customization.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Input Your Prompt" icon="keyboard">
    Guide the AI by inserting a **descriptive prompt**, crucial for achieving results that align with your creative goals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Adjust Output Settings" icon="sliders">
    Fine-tune settings like **strength**, **guiding scale**, and **numb interference steps** for your output image generation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Draw or Upload Image" icon="pencil">
    Start by **creating a square** using your toolbar. Then begin drawing or upload an image you'd like to enhance or modify.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Enhance with Colors" icon="fill-drip">
    Use the **DrawFast icon** to add colors to your creation, selecting from a wide palette to make your image pop.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 6: Download or Animate" icon="download">
    Once satisfied, **download your image** or explore the option to **animate your creation** for added dimension.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
