# Flux In Context: Seamless Logo Placement on Products

<Tip>
  Effortlessly place your logo onto any product or scene using Flux In Context! Achieve consistent branding and realistic placements with the power of AI.
</Tip>

## Overview

Flux In Context is a specialized AI tool designed to solve the common challenge of visualizing how a logo appears on a product or within a specific environment. By combining your logo image with a descriptive destination prompt and an optional logo description, this tool leverages advanced AI to seamlessly integrate your logo into a generated image, making it appear naturally on the intended surface. It's particularly useful for branding, marketing mockups, and creative visualizations.

## How to Use Flux In Context

Follow these steps to place your logo onto a product or scene:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your Logo" icon="upload">
    Upload the image file of the **logo** you want to place. Ensure the logo is clear and has a transparent or easily removable background for best results.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe Your Logo (Optional)" icon="pencil">
    Provide a detailed text description of your logo. This helps the AI understand the logo's features, colors, and style, leading to more consistent placement and appearance in the final image.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Define the Destination" icon="map">
    Enter a **destination prompt** describing the product or scene where you want your logo to appear. Be specific about the object and its context.
    
    <Callout type="example">
      *Example Destination Prompts:*
      - "a white ceramic coffee mug on a wooden table"
      - "a black t-shirt hanging on a rack in a clothing store"
      - "a laptop screen in a modern office environment"
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Image" icon="play">
    Click the "Generate" button. The AI will process your logo, description, and destination prompt to create a new image with your logo placed in the specified location.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Input Parameters

Here are the key input parameters for Flux In Context:

<ResponseField name="logo_image" type="file" required>
  The image file of the logo you want to place.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="logo_description" type="string">
  A detailed text description of the logo for better consistency in placement and appearance. (Optional but recommended)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="destination_prompt" type="string" required>
  A text prompt describing the product or scene where the logo should be placed.
</ResponseField>
