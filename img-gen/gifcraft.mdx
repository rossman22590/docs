# GifCraft: Create Custom GIFs

<Tip>
  Create hilarious and personalized GIFs with GifCraft! Swap faces, add your own images, and share your creations with the world.
</Tip>

## Overview

GifCraft is a fun and easy-to-use tool that allows you to create custom GIFs by swapping faces with your favorite characters. Powered by Giphy, GifCraft gives you access to a massive library of GIFs, as well as the ability to upload your own.

## How to Use GifCraft

Creating your own custom GIF is a simple two-step process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Select a Face Image">
    Select an image of a face you want to insert into the GIF. Make sure the face is clear and visible for best results.
    <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/gifcraft2.png" alt="GifCraft Step 1" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Choose a GIF">
    Choose a GIF from the Giphy library or upload your own file with a .gif extension. Make sure the GIF has a visible face to replace for best results.
    <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/gifcraft1.png" alt="GifCraft Step 2" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Conclusion

GifCraft is a powerful and easy-to-use tool that allows you to create custom GIFs in just a few simple steps. Whether you're looking to create a hilarious meme or a personalized message, GifCraft has you covered.
