### ImgCraft on Pixio offers unparalleled control and flexibility in AI image generation, catering to both professionals and enthusiasts who demand precision and quality.

With unique features like model size selection, multiple model integration, and image upscaling, ImgCraft empowers users to create diverse, high-quality images tailored to their exact specifications.

#### Key Features of ImgCraft
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Model Size Selection" icon="arrows-left-right-to-line">
    Choose between **1X or XL Stable Diffusion models**, allowing customization between speed and detail in generated images.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multiple Model Integration" icon="layer-group">
    Select **more than one model** simultaneously, creating a blend of styles and qualities for richer and more varied outputs.

  <Card title="Image Upscaling" icon="up-right-and-down-left-from-center">

  <Card title="Advanced Customization" icon="sliders">

#### How to Use ImgCraft

Follow these steps to harness the full power of ImgCraft and create highly customized, high-quality AI-generated images.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Model Selection" icon="cubes">
    Start by choosing the desired **model size(s)** from the 1X and XL options or combine models for a diverse effect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Input Prompts and Customize Settings" icon="pen-to-square">
    Enter both **positive and negative prompts** to guide the AI. Further customize with settings like **aspect ratio**, **seed**, and the inclusion of **loras** under the 'Extras' button for nuanced adjustments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Generate and Upscale" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    After setting your preferences, generate your image. Utilize the **upscaling feature** post-generation to boost image quality, making it suitable for various uses.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Refinement and Finalization" icon="check-double">
    The combination of selected models, detailed prompts, and subsequent upscaling will produce a **high-quality, finely-tuned output** that aligns with your creative vision.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Tips for Success with ImgCraft

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Balance Between Model Choices" icon="scale-balanced">
    Consider the unique advantages of the **1X and XL models** in relation to your project's needs for speed versus detail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Judicious Use of Upscaling" icon="up-right-and-down-left-from-center">
    While upscaling can significantly improve image quality, use this feature wisely to **avoid potential artifacts**. Experiment to find the appropriate balance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Explore Multiple Models" icon="layer-group">
    The innovative capability to use more than one model can unlock creative potentials that a single model approach might not achieve. **Experiment with various combinations** for unique outcomes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fine-tune with Advanced Settings" icon="sliders">
    Make use of the **advanced customization options** like loras and aspect ratio adjustments to achieve precise results that match your vision.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
