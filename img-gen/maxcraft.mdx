## How to Use MaxCraft

Follow these simple steps to generate realistic images quickly:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Your Prompt" icon="keyboard">
    Type a text description of the image you want to create in the prompt input field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Aspect Ratio" icon="crop">
    Choose the desired shape for your image from the available aspect ratio options.
    *   Possible values: `1:1`, `16:9`, `4:3`, `3:2`, `2:3`, `3:4`, `9:16`, `21:9`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Number of Images" icon="clone">
    Specify how many variations of the image you want to generate (1-9).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Image" icon="play">
    Click the "Generate" button. MaxCraft will process your inputs and create realistic images.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Input Parameters and Options

MaxCraft offers a streamlined set of input parameters for quick and simple generation:

<ResponseField name="prompt" type="string" required>
  Your text description guiding the image creation.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="aspect_ratio" type="Enum" required>
  The desired aspect ratio for the output image.
  *   Possible enum values: `1:1`, `16:9`, `4:3`, `3:2`, `2:3`, `3:4`, `9:16`, `21:9`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="num_images" type="integer">
  The number of images to generate in a single batch.
  *   Default: 1
  *   Range: 1-9
</ResponseField>


## Mastering Prompts for MaxCraft

To get the best realistic images from MaxCraft, focus on descriptive prompts that guide the AI towards lifelike visuals.

### Prompting Techniques:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Emphasize Realism" icon="camera">
    Use keywords like "photorealistic," "realistic," "high detail," "ultra sharp details," and describe textures and materials accurately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Detail Lighting" icon="sun">
    Describe the lighting conditions precisely (e.g., "golden hour," "studio lighting," "dramatic contrast").
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Specify Camera Details (Optional)" icon="camera">
    Including camera type, lens, or settings can sometimes influence the final look for photorealistic styles.
    *Example:* "shot with Canon EOS R3, 50mm f/1.2 RF lens."
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Describe Scene and Subject Clearly" icon="keyboard">
    Provide clear descriptions of the environment, subjects, and their actions or poses.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
