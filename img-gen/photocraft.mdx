# PhotoCraft: Consistent Subject Generation in Any Scene

<Tip>
  Bring your characters to life in endless scenarios with PhotoCraft! Generate multiple images of a specific person or character, maintaining their likeness while exploring different poses and backgrounds.
</Tip>

## Overview

PhotoCraft is a specialized AI tool designed to generate consistent images of a particular subject (person or character) across various scenes and poses. You start by uploading an image of your subject, and then use text prompts and settings to guide the AI in creating new photographs featuring that same subject. This tool is invaluable for creating visual narratives, character sheets, or generating diverse marketing materials featuring a consistent individual.

## How to Use PhotoCraft

Follow these steps to generate consistent images of your subject in various scenarios:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Subject Image" icon="upload">
    Upload a clear image of the **person or character** you want to generate images of. A headshot or portrait is often a good starting point.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Prompts" icon="keyboard">
    Provide a **Prompt** describing the desired scene, pose, or style for the generated image. Optionally, use a **Negative Prompt** to specify elements to avoid.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust Settings" icon="sliders">
    Configure the generation parameters to fine-tune your output:
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Output Quality" icon="star">
        Adjust a slider (0-100) to control the overall quality of the generated images. Higher quality may increase generation time.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Number of Images" icon="clone">
        Select how many images you want to generate in a single batch (1-4).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Output Format" icon="file-image">
        Choose the file format for your generated images (PNG, JPG, WEBP).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Seed" icon="seedling">
        Enter a specific number for reproducible results. Using the same seed, prompt, and settings will produce the same image(s). Leave empty for random results.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Randomize Poses" icon="shuffle">
        Toggle this switch to generate images with randomized poses for your subject. Disable it if you want more control over the pose via the prompt.
      </Accordion>
      {/* <Accordion title="Images per Pose" icon="image">
        Select how many images to generate for each pose (1-2). (Note: This option may not be visible in the current UI).
      </Accordion> */}
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Images" icon="play">
    Click the "Generate" button. PhotoCraft will process your inputs and create images of your subject in the described scenarios and poses.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Download" icon="eye">
    Examine the generated images in the gallery. You can download individual images or all images as a ZIP file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Input Parameters and Options

PhotoCraft offers a range of input parameters to control the consistent subject generation process:

<ResponseField name="inputImage" type="file" required>
  The image file of the subject (person or character) you want to generate images of.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="prompt" type="string" required>
  Your text description of the desired scene, pose, or style for the generated images.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="negativePrompt" type="string">
  A text prompt specifying elements or characteristics to avoid in the generated images.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="outputQuality" type="integer">
  Controls the overall quality of the generated images.
  *   Default: 80
  *   Range: 0-100
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="numberOfOutputs" type="integer">
  The number of images to generate in a single batch.
  *   Default: 2
  *   Range: 1-4
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="outputFormat" type="Enum" required>
  The file format for the generated images.
  *   Possible enum values: `png`, `jpg`, `webp`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="randomizePoses" type="boolean">
  Toggle to randomize the poses of the subject in the generated images.
  *   Default: `true`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="seed" type="integer">
  A number that initializes the random generation process. Using the same seed with the same prompt and settings will produce consistent results. Leave empty for a random seed.
</ResponseField>

## Tips for Best Results

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Clear Subject Image" icon="user">
    Use a clear, well-lit image of your subject where their face and features are easily visible.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Describe the Scene & Pose" icon="keyboard">
    Provide detailed prompts describing the desired background, environment, and the pose you want your subject to be in (especially if Randomize Poses is off).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Experiment with Quality" icon="sliders">
    Adjust the Output Quality slider to find the balance between detail and generation time that suits your needs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Utilize Randomize Poses" icon="shuffle">
    Toggle Randomize Poses on to quickly explore a variety of poses for your subject in the described scene.
  </Card>
   <Card title="Use Seed for Consistency" icon="seedling">
    If you find a result you like, note the seed used so you can regenerate similar images or make small adjustments while keeping the subject consistent.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Conclusion

PhotoCraft offers a powerful and intuitive way to generate consistent images of a specific subject in various scenarios and poses. By simply uploading a subject image and providing prompts, you can create diverse visual content while maintaining the unique likeness of your chosen person or character.
