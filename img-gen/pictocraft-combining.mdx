### PictoCraft's Combining Images feature offers a powerful and intuitive way to merge multiple images, allowing you to create unique and customized visuals with precision and creativity.

This versatile tool enables you to blend up to four images, adjust their influence, and apply various combination methods to achieve your desired aesthetic.

#### Key Features of Combining Images
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Multiple Image Input" icon="images">
    Upload and combine **up to 4 images** for complex visual compositions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Advanced Adjustments" icon="sliders">
    Fine-tune the **weight and stop-at values** for each image to control their contribution to the final result.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Various Combination Methods" icon="layer-group">
    Choose from methods like **Image Prompt**, **Pyracanny**, **CPDS**, and **Faceswap** for diverse effects.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customizable Image Prompts" icon="keyboard">
    Enter specific prompts to guide the generation of your combined image.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

#### How to Use Combining Images

Follow these steps to effectively combine multiple images in PictoCraft.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Enable Input Image Box" icon="check-square">
    Check off the **input image box** to activate the image combination options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Access Image Prompt" icon="image">
    Select **"Image Prompt"** from the toolbar to open multiple panels for image input.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Input Multiple Images" icon="upload">
    Upload up to **4 images** into the provided panels for combination.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Adjust Advanced Options" icon="cogs">
    Check the **advanced box** to reveal additional options under each image panel. Use sliders to adjust **weight and stop-at values** for each image.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Select Combination Method" icon="magic">
    Choose from options like **Image Prompt**, **Pyracanny**, **CPDS**, or **Faceswap** based on your creative goal.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 6: Set and Review Your Images" icon="eye">
    Enter an **image prompt** to generate the combined image and review the result.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
