### PictoCraft on Pixio offers a comprehensive suite of AI-powered image generation and manipulation tools, catering to both novices and professionals in the realm of digital creativity.

This versatile platform transforms textual prompts into vivid, detailed images and provides innovative options for refining and customizing outputs, pushing the boundaries of AI-assisted creativity.

#### How to Use PictoCraft

Follow these steps to harness the full power of PictoCraft for your creative projects.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Enter Your Prompt" icon="keyboard">
    Start by typing in your **textual prompt**, detailing the scene, object, or concept you wish to visualize.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Upload Images (Optional)" icon="upload">
    Click on the **input image box** to upload images that can refine your image generation process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Select Input Option" icon="list-ul">
    Choose from options like **Upscale**, **PyraCanny**, **CPDS**, or **Image Prompt** based on your creative needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Configure Advanced Settings" icon="cogs">
    Click on **'Advanced'** to access and adjust output settings, controlling aspects like model, style, and aspect ratios.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Generate and Review" icon="eye">
    Proceed to **generate your image**, review the output, and make any necessary adjustments to perfect your creation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Specialized Input Options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Upscale" icon="expand">
    Elevate the **resolution of your images**, bringing unparalleled clarity and detail to every pixel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="PyraCanny" icon="bezier-curve">
    Alter **shapes within your images**, offering a unique way to reinterpret and stylize your visuals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="CPDS" icon="adjust">

  <Accordion title="Image Prompt" icon="layer-group">
    Select **multiple images** to copy and create styles based on inserted visuals, enabling unique blends and fusions.

</AccordionGroup>
