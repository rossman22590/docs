### PictoCraft's In Painting feature combines inpainting and outpainting tools, allowing you to modify specific areas of your images with unprecedented precision and creativity.

This powerful feature enables you to enhance, transform, or correct parts of your image, bringing your creative vision to life with flexibility and control.

#### Key Features of In Painting
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Inpainting" icon="brush">
    Modify **specific areas within your image**, perfect for removing unwanted elements or adding new details.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outpainting" icon="expand">
    Extend your image beyond its original boundaries in any direction (**left, right, top, bottom**).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Precise Area Selection" icon="crop">
    Use the drawing tool to **highlight exact areas** for modification.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customizable Settings" icon="sliders">
    Adjust additional settings to fine-tune your inpainting or outpainting process.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

#### How to Use In Painting

Follow these steps to effectively use the inpainting and outpainting features in PictoCraft.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Input an Image" icon="file-image">
    Upload the image you wish to modify as the base for your inpainting or outpainting process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Select Inpaint or Outpaint Option" icon="paint-brush">
    Choose the **"Inpaint or Outpaint" option** from the toolbar to activate the modification tools.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Draw the Area to Modify" icon="pencil-alt">
    Use the drawing tool to **highlight the specific area** of the image you want to change.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Choose the Target Area" icon="expand-arrows-alt">
    For outpainting, specify the location by clicking on one or more boxes (**Left, Right, Bottom, Top**) under your image.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Adjust Additional Settings" icon="sliders">
    Use the dropdown menu next to the outpainting direction options to **choose the best-suited option** for your modification purpose.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 6: Input Your Prompt" icon="keyboard">
    Enter your prompt, including the **modifications you want to make**, to generate the new image.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Tips for Best Results with In Painting

<AccordionGroup>

#### Why Use In Painting?

PictoCraft's In Painting feature offers a powerful toolset for precise image modification, providing several key benefits:

- **Flexibility**: Modify specific areas within your image or extend it beyond its original boundaries.
- **Precision**: The drawing tool allows for exact selection of areas to be modified.
- **Creativity**: Combine inpainting and outpainting to transform your images in unique ways.
- **Control**: Fine-tune your modifications with customizable settings and detailed prompts.
- **Versatility**: Suitable for various tasks, from removing unwanted elements to adding new details or expanding scenes.

Whether you're a professional designer looking to make precise edits, a digital artist exploring creative possibilities, or simply someone wanting to enhance personal photos, In Painting in PictoCraft provides the tools you need to bring your creative vision to life with unprecedented control and flexibility.
