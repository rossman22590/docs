### PictoCraft on Pixio offers unparalleled control and customization in AI-powered image generation, perfect for detailed project requirements and professional design work.

With its extensive range of adjustable parameters, PictoCraft empowers users to refine their creations beyond simple prompts, enabling a higher degree of precision in the final output.

#### Key Features of PictoCraft
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Positive and Negative Prompts" icon="pen-fancy">
    Guide the AI with descriptions of what you want and **don't want** in your generated images for more precise outcomes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Base Model Selection" icon="cubes">
    Choose a **foundational style or approach** for the AI to follow in generating images.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance Customization" icon="gauge-high">
    Prioritize **quality or speed** in the image generation process, catering to specific project needs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Advanced Settings" icon="sliders">
    Specify **aspect ratio**, **seed**, **number of images**, **sharpness**, and **guidance scale** for unprecedented control over the AI's creative process.
  </Card>

#### How to Use PictoCraft

Follow these steps to harness the full power of PictoCraft and create highly customized AI-generated images.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Access PictoCraft" icon="arrow-pointer">
    Click on the dropdown menu at the top of the page to find and select PictoCraft.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Input Prompts" icon="keyboard">
    Enter both your **positive and negative prompts** to guide the AI on what to include and exclude in the generated images.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Customize Settings" icon="sliders">
    Choose your **base model**, decide whether to prioritize quality or speed, and adjust the **aspect ratio** and **seed** according to your project needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Advanced Adjustments" icon="gears">
    Fine-tune your results by specifying the **number of images**, adjusting **sharpness**, and setting the **guidance scale**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Style Addition" icon="palette">
    Use the 'Add' button to incorporate a specific style into your image generation, further refining the final product.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 6: Generate" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    With all settings customized to your preference, proceed to generate your images.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Tips for Success with PictoCraft

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Utilize Both Prompts" icon="pen-fancy">
    Make full use of **positive and negative prompts** to narrow down the AI's focus and avoid undesired elements in your images.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Balance Performance and Needs" icon="scale-balanced">
    Consider the trade-off between **quality and speed** based on your project deadlines and the level of detail required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Experiment with Styles" icon="palette">
    Don't shy away from experimenting with different styles to discover unique and compelling visual outcomes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Document Your Seeds" icon="seedling">
    If you achieve particularly successful results, note down the **seed used** to replicate the same conditions for future projects.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
