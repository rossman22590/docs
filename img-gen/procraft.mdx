### ProCraft on Pixio offers enhanced control and specificity in AI image generation, building upon PictoCraft's foundation with additional features for fine-tuning and detail work.

Ideal for projects requiring attention to fine details, ProCraft introduces specialized tools like face restoration and tiling, empowering users to create highly customized and precise AI-generated images.

#### Key Features of ProCraft
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Comprehensive Customization" icon="sliders">
    Retain **customizable settings** from PictoCraft, including positive and negative prompts, base model selection, and seed input for a fully tailored experience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Restore Faces" icon="face-smile">
    Enhance and restore **faces in generated images**, ideal for projects involving portraits or characters, ensuring clear and natural-looking facial representations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tiling" icon="border-all">
    Create images that can be **tiled seamlessly**, perfect for backgrounds, textures, or any content requiring a continuous pattern without visible seams.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Loras and Embeddings" icon="layer-group">
    Access advanced options like **loras and embeddings** under the 'Extras' button, providing subtle yet impactful ways to influence the AI's output.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

#### How to Use ProCraft

Follow these steps to harness the full power of ProCraft and create highly customized, detailed AI-generated images.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Input Prompts and Customize" icon="pen-to-square">
    Begin by entering your **positive and negative prompts**. Customize the base model, Sampler, size, and other basic settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Utilize Advanced Features" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Select the **'Restore Faces'** option for projects involving human characters or portraits. Use the **'Tiling'** feature for projects requiring seamless patterns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Access Extras for Depth" icon="layer-group">
    Click on the **'Extras' button** to explore additional settings like loras and embeddings. These tools offer deeper customization and can significantly impact the AI's creative direction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Fine-Tune and Generate" icon="image">
    With all settings adjusted to your preference, proceed to generate your images. ProCraft will utilize the detailed parameters to produce highly customized and precise outputs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
