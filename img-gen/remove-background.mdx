#### How to Use Remove Background

Follow these simple steps to remove backgrounds from your images using the Remove Background tool.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Upload Your Image" icon="upload">
    Start by **uploading the image** from which you wish to remove the background. Ensure the subject is clearly visible for optimal results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Remove Background" icon="eraser">
    With your image uploaded, simply click the **'Remove Background' button**. The tool will automatically process your image, identifying and isolating the subject.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Review and Download" icon="eye">
    Once the background removal process is complete, **review your image** to ensure satisfaction with the result. Then **download the image** with its transparent or plain background.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Tips for Best Results with Remove Background

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Select Clear Images" icon="image">
    Use images where the **subject is clearly defined** and contrasted against the background for more accurate AI processing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review Results Carefully" icon="search">
    After background removal, ensure that **edges around the subject are clean** and no parts of the subject have been mistakenly removed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Consider Lighting and Contrast" icon="adjust">
    Images with **good lighting and clear contrast** between subject and background tend to yield better results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Prepare for Various Applications" icon="th-large">
    Consider how you'll use the image and ensure the removal meets the needs of your specific application, whether for web design, print, or digital media.

</AccordionGroup>
