#### How to Use Restore Face

Follow these steps to enhance and refine the faces in your photographs using Restore Face.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Choose a File" icon="file-image">
    Click the **'Choose File' button** to select the photo you wish to enhance from your device.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Adjust Fidelity Factor" icon="sliders">
    Use the **'Fidelity Factor' slider** to set the level of detail you wish to restore. Higher values increase emphasis on facial details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Set Upscale Factor" icon="up-right-and-down-left-from-center">
    Increase the **'Upscale Factor'** up to a maximum of 4 to enlarge the image without losing quality.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Toggle Background Enhancement" icon="image">
    Enable the **'Background Enhance' button** if you wish to improve the clarity of the photo's background.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Apply Face Upsampling" icon="magnifying-glass-plus">
    Use the **'Face Up sample' feature** to specifically improve the resolution of faces in your images.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 6: Initiate Restoration" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Click the **'Restore Face' button** to start the restoration process, applying all selected enhancements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 7: Download Enhanced Image" icon="download">
    After restoration is complete, use the **'Download Image' button** to save the final result to your device.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
