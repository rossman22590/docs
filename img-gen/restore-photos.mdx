#### How to Use Restore Photos

Follow these simple steps to bring your old, weathered photos back to life.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Select Your Photo" icon="file-image">
    Click the **'Choose File' button** to open a dialog box and select the photo you want to restore from your computer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Enhance Resolution (Optional)" icon="up-right-and-down-left-from-center">
    Switch on the **'High Resolution' toggle** for a clearer, more detailed image, especially useful for large prints or displays.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Remove Scratches (Optional)" icon="eraser">
    If your photo has scratches or marks, turn on the **'Remove Scratches' toggle** to instruct the AI to repair these imperfections.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Initiate Restoration" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Click the **'Restore Image' button** to start the AI-powered restoration process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Download Your Restored Photo" icon="download">
    After restoration is complete, use the **'Download Image' button** to save the restored photo to your device.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
