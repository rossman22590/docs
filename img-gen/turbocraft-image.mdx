#### How to Use TurboCraft Image

Follow these simple steps to generate stylized images using TurboCraft Image and see your ideas come to life with a unique artistic flair.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Start with an Image" icon="image">
    Begin by **uploading an image** that captures the style you wish to emulate. This could be anything from a classic painting to a modern graphic design.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Craft Your Prompt" icon="keyboard">
    As you type your prompt, TurboCraft Image **dynamically generates visuals** that mirror the style of your uploaded image, creating bespoke images that are both relevant to your prompt and stylistically consistent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Adjust the Strength Control" icon="sliders">
    Use the **strength control bar** to dictate the AI's level of creativity. Set it lower for more straightforward generations or higher for more abstract and unique interpretations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Explore Your Generation History" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    To the right of the generated image, find a comprehensive **history of all images produced** during your session. Each image in your history is downloadable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Manage Your History Visibility" icon="eye">
    Toggle the "show history" option to **display or hide your generation history** based on your workflow preferences.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
