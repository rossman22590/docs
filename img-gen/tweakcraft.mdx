### TweakCraft on Pixio leverages Stable Diffusion technology to offer powerful image remixing and modification capabilities, allowing users to refine, alter, or creatively transform their visual content.

This tool is ideal for photographers, designers, and anyone looking to enhance their images through AI-assisted editing, providing a sophisticated platform for detailed adjustments and creative remixes.

#### Key Features of TweakCraft
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stable Diffusion Integration" icon="atom">
    Harness the power of **Stable Diffusion** for precise and creative image modifications.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customizable Prompts" icon="comment">
    Use **positive and negative prompts** to guide the AI in implementing desired changes while preserving specific aspects.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Model Configuration Options" icon="microchip">
    Choose from various **Stable Diffusion model configurations** to suit your specific style or detail requirements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Adjustable Remixing Settings" icon="sliders">
    Fine-tune the tweaking process with settings for **sampler**, **denoising strength**, **steps**, and **CFG scale**.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

#### How to Use TweakCraft

Follow these steps to remix and modify your images using TweakCraft.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Access TweakCraft" icon="arrow-pointer">
    Locate TweakCraft under the **dropdown menu** at the top of the Pixio page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 2: Upload or Input Image" icon="upload">
    Start by **uploading the image** you want to tweak or providing its URL directly into TweakCraft.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 3: Craft Your Prompts" icon="pen-fancy">
    Enter a **positive prompt** detailing desired changes and a **negative prompt** to specify aspects to preserve.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 4: Select Model Configuration" icon="cubes">
    Choose the specific **Stable Diffusion model configuration** that best suits your remix goals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 5: Adjust Remixing Settings" icon="sliders">
    Tailor the process by selecting a **sampler**, adjusting **denoising strength**, setting **number of steps**, and modifying **CFG scale**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 6: Generate and Review" icon="eye">
    Initiate the remixing process and review the resulting image that aligns with your vision.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Tips for Best Results with TweakCraft

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Craft Detailed Prompts" icon="pencil">
    Be as **specific as possible** in your prompts to effectively guide the AI and achieve desired results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Experiment with Models and Settings" icon="flask">

</AccordionGroup>
