## How to Use Style Transfer

Follow these steps to transform your images:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Base Image">
    Upload the image you wish to transform. This will be the foundation for the style transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Input a Prompt (Optional)">
    Add a simple text prompt to guide the style transfer process.
    
    <Callout type="tip">
      A simple prompt is often sufficient. No need for elaborate descriptions.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Style Image">
    Upload the image whose style you want to apply. Images with clear edges and distinct styles work best.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Style and Structure Strengths">
    Use the sliders to fine-tune your result:
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Style Strength" icon="brush">
        Controls how much of the style image's artistic elements are applied.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Structure Strength" icon="layer-group">
        Determines how much of the base image's original form is preserved.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://docs.myapps.ai/storage/page_media/cfdaffcc-9e4a-43f2-a12b-ea881b684d68.png" alt="Style Transfer Controls" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upscale and Enhance">
    Initiate the process to apply style transfer and enhance resolution.
    
    <Callout type="info">
      Image generation may take up to a few minutes. Please be patient during processing.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Your Masterpiece">
    Once complete, download your transformed image for sharing or further editing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
