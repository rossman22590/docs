## How to Use Upscale HD

Follow these steps to upscale your images with high quality and creative control:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Image" icon="upload">
    Upload the image file you want to upscale.
    
    <Callout type="info">
      The tool compresses the image for processing.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Scale Factor" icon="maximize">
    Choose the desired scale factor for upscaling (2x, 4x, or 8x).
    
    <Callout type="warning">
      Each scale factor requires a specific number of credits.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Optimization" icon="check">
    Choose what the upscaling is optimized for (e.g., Standard, Soft Portraits, Art & Illustration).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Prompt" icon="keyboard">
    Provide a text prompt to guide the upscaling process. This helps the AI understand the content and add details consistent with your description.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust Creative Settings (Optional)" icon="sliders">
    Configure optional parameters to influence the creative aspects of the upscale:
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Creativity" icon="palette">
        Slider (-10 to 10) to influence the AI's creative interpretation during upscaling.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="HDR" icon="sun">
        Slider (-10 to 10) to adjust the High Dynamic Range effect.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Resemblance" icon="copy">
        Slider (-10 to 10) to control how much the upscaled image resembles the original.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Fractality" icon="code-branch">
        Slider (-10 to 10) to influence fractal-like patterns or complexity.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Engine" icon="microchip">
    Choose the Magnific engine to use for upscaling.
    *   Possible values: `Automatic`, `Magnific Illusio`, `Magnific Sharpy`, `Magnific Sparkle`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upscale Image" icon="play">
    Click the "Upscale Image" button. The tool will process your image based on the selected settings.
    
    <Callout type="info">
      The upscaling process may take some time depending on the scale factor and complexity.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Input Parameters and Options

Upscale HD offers a range of input parameters for controlling the upscaling process:

<ResponseField name="image" type="file" required>
  The image file to be upscaled. (Compressed for processing).
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="scale_factor" type="Enum" required>
  The factor to which the image will be upscaled.
  *   Possible enum values: `2x`, `4x`, `8x`.
  *   Credits required: 40 (2x), 80 (4x), 160 (8x).
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="optimized_for" type="Enum" required>
  Specifies what the upscaling is optimized for.
  *   Possible enum values: `standard`, `soft_portraits`, `hard_portraits`, `art_n_illustration`, `videogame_assets`, `nature_n_landscapes`, `films_n_photography`, `3d_renders`, `science_fiction_n_horror`.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="prompt" type="string" required>
  A text prompt to guide the upscaling process and add details.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="creativity" type="integer">
  Influences the AI's creative interpretation during upscaling.
  *   Default: 0
  *   Range: -10 to 10
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="hdr" type="integer">
  Adjusts the High Dynamic Range effect.
  *   Default: 0
  *   Range: -10 to 10
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="resemblance" type="integer">
  Controls how much the upscaled image resembles the original.
  *   Default: 0
  *   Range: -10 to 10
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="fractality" type="integer">
  Influences fractal-like patterns or complexity.
  *   Default: 0
  *   Range: -10 to 10
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="engine" type="Enum">
  Selects the Magnific engine to use.
  *   Default: `automatic`
  *   Possible enum values: `automatic`, `magnific_illusio`, `magnific_sharpy`, `magnific_sparkle`.
</ResponseField>
