## 1. Overview

Video Sound FX is an innovative tool designed to enhance your videos with custom-generated sound effects. This application uses advanced AI technology to analyze your video content and create appropriate sound effects, adding a new dimension to your visual storytelling.

## 2. Getting Started

To begin using Video Sound FX, navigate to the main dashboard. Here, you'll find options to start a new project or continue working on existing ones.

## 3. Uploading Your Video

The first step in using Video Sound FX is to upload your video file.

![Upload Video Interface](https://img.mytsi.org/i/d2Zz726.png)

- Click on the "Upload Video" button or drag and drop your video file into the designated area
- Supported video formats include MP4, AVI, MOV, and WMV
- A progress bar will indicate the upload status

## 4. Generating Sound Effects

Once your video is uploaded, Video Sound FX will analyze the content and generate four different sound effect options.

![Sound Effects Options](https://img.mytsi.org/i/mBHj725.png)

- After processing, you'll be presented with four unique sound effect tracks
- Listen to each option by clicking the play button next to it
- Choose the sound effect that best fits your video by selecting the corresponding option
- You can preview how the selected sound effect works with your video before finalizing

## 5. Best Practices

To get the most out of Video Sound FX, consider the following best practices:

### 5.1 Video Simplicity

- Keep your video content simple and focused
- Complex scenes with multiple actions may result in less accurate sound effects

### 5.2 Optimal Video Length

- For best results, use videos that are under 10 seconds long
- Shorter videos allow the AI to focus on specific actions and generate more accurate sound effects

### 5.3 Clear Action Sequences

- Ensure that the main actions in your video are clearly visible
- This helps the AI accurately interpret the scene and generate appropriate sound effects

### 5.4 Good Video Quality

- Use high-quality video footage for better analysis
- Clear, well-lit scenes provide better input for the AI to work with

## 6. Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using Video Sound FX, try the following:

- Ensure your video file is in a supported format
- Check your internet connection for smooth uploading and processing
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- If problems persist, contact our support team

## 7. FAQ

**Q: What video formats are supported?**
A: Video Sound FX supports MP4, AVI, MOV, and WMV formats.

**Q: Is there a limit to the video length?**
A: While there's no strict limit, we recommend using videos under 10 seconds for optimal results.

**Q: Can I use copyrighted music in my videos?**
A: Video Sound FX generates original sound effects. For any background music, ensure you have the necessary rights or licenses.

**Q: How long does it take to generate sound effects?**
A: Processing time varies depending on video length and complexity, but typically takes a few minutes.

**Q: Can I edit the generated sound effects?**
A: Currently, Video Sound FX doesn't offer editing capabilities. You can choose from the four generated options.
