## 1. Overview

Beam + Gemini is an advanced AI interaction tool that builds upon the capabilities of AI Tutor Plus. It introduces the powerful Beam functionality, allowing users to chat with multiple Language Learning Models (LLMs) simultaneously, and integrates the cutting-edge Gemini 1.5 Pro LLM with its impressive 2 million token context window.

## 13. FAQ

**Q: How does Beam differ from regular chat interactions?**
A: Beam allows you to interact with multiple AI models simultaneously, providing diverse perspectives and insights on a single query.

**Q: Can I use Gemini 1.5 Pro with other models in Beam?**
A: Yes, Gemini 1.5 Pro can be used alongside other models in Beam for comprehensive analysis.

**Q: How do I create a custom persona?**
A: Use the Persona Creator tool to generate prompts from text or YouTube videos, then apply them to your chat interactions.

**Q: What's the advantage of the 2 million token context window in Gemini 1.5 Pro?**
A: It allows for processing and analysis of much larger amounts of text, enabling more complex and context-rich interactions.
