# Animate Portrait: Breathe Life into Still Images

<Tip>
  Transform static portraits into dynamic, expressive animations with our advanced AI-driven facial animation technology!
</Tip>

## Overview

Animate Portrait is a powerful tool that allows you to bring still images to life by mapping facial expressions and movements from a source video onto a target image. This technology opens up new possibilities for creating engaging content without the need for complex equipment or 3D facial capture systems.

## How to Use Animate Portrait

Follow these steps to animate your portrait:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Source Video" icon="film">
    Upload an MP4 video up to 30 seconds long. This video will drive the facial expressions of your target image.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://img.mytsi.org/i/BmtY811.png" alt="Source Video Upload Interface" />
    </Frame>
    
    <Callout type="tip">
      For best results, ensure the subject in the source video is centered in the frame and well-aligned.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Target Image" icon="image">
    Upload the portrait image you want to animate. This image will be brought to life using the expressions from the source video.
    
    <Frame>

    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with Animate Portrait, try these solutions:

1. **Poor Animation Quality**: Ensure both your source video and target image are high-quality and well-aligned.
2. **Mismatched Expressions**: Try using a source video with more exaggerated facial movements.
3. **Artifacts or Glitches**: Check that your target image has a clear, unobstructed view of the face.
4. **Processing Errors**: If the animation fails, try re-uploading your files or using different source/target combinations.
