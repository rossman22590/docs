FaceSwap Video is a cool tool that lets you switch faces in videos! It's perfect for when you want to add a twist to your clips by putting a different face onto someone in the video. Whether you're just having fun, creating a meme, or trying out a new look, FaceSwap Video makes it super easy.

**How It Works**
----------------

Pick Your Video:

*   Choose a short video, 20 seconds or less, where you want the face swap to happen.
    

Choose a Face:

*   Select a photo with the face you want to use. This could be your face, a friend's, or even a celebrity's â€“ as long as you have the right to use it.
    

Swap It:

*   Upload both the video and the photo with the face to FaceSwap Video. The tool will take care of the rest, swapping the face from your photo onto the face in the video.
    

And just like that, you've got a brand new video with a face swap that will make everyone do a double-take. Share it with friends, post it online, or keep it for a good laugh. FaceSwap Video is all about making video editing fun and simple for everyone.
