# FilmCraft: AI Video Generation

<Tip>
  Unlock the power of CogVideo with FilmCraft! Create stunning videos from text, images, and even other videos with this powerful AI tool.
</Tip>

## Overview

FilmCraft is a cutting-edge AI platform that leverages the powerful **CogVideo model** for video generation. With its ability to produce high-quality videos and handle a wide range of styles, FilmCraft is the perfect tool for creating posters, infographics, and other professional-quality visuals.

## Modes of Operation

FilmCraft offers three primary modes of operation, each designed for a specific video creation workflow.

### Text to Video

In Text to Video mode, you can create new videos from scratch using text prompts.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Parameters" icon="sliders">
    - **Prompt**: Your text description of the desired video.
    - **Negative Prompt**: Specify elements to omit from the video.
    - **Video Size**: Choose from a variety of preset sizes or specify a custom resolution.
    - **Inference Steps**: The number of steps to take when generating the video.
    - **Guidance Scale**: Dictates how closely the image generation follows your prompt.
    - **Use RIFE**: Enable to use RIFE for video interpolation.
    - **Export FPS**: The frames per second of the output video.
    - **LoRAs**: Add custom LoRAs to create unique and personalized videos.
    - **Seed**: Use a seed to control the output. The same seed + same prompt will produce an identical output.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
