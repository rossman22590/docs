# FxCraft: AI Video Generation

<Tip>
  Unlock the power of PixVerse with FxCraft! Create stunning videos from text and images with this powerful AI tool.
</Tip>

## Overview

FxCraft is a game-changing AI video generator that turns text prompts and images into vibrant videos in seconds, no editing skills required. Powered by PixVerse, FxCraft makes video creation accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill or experience.

## Modes of Operation

FxCraft offers five primary modes of operation, each designed for a specific video creation workflow.

### Standard Video

In Standard Video mode, you can create new videos from scratch using text prompts.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Parameters" icon="sliders">
    - **Prompt**: Your text description of the desired video.
    - **Negative Prompt**: Specify elements to omit from the video.
    - **Duration**: Choose between 5 and 8 seconds.
    - **Quality**: Choose between 360p, 540p, 720p, and 1080p.
    - **Motion Mode**: Choose between normal and fast.
    - **Aspect Ratio**: Choose between 16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:3, and 3:4.
    - **Style**: Choose between anime, 3d_animation, comic, cyberpunk, and clay.
    - **Seed**: Use a seed to control the output. The same seed + same prompt will produce an identical output.
    - **Camera Movement**: Choose between horizontal_left, horizontal_right, vertical_up, vertical_down, zoom_in, and zoom_out.
    - **Auto Sound**: Automatically add sound to your video.
    - **Sound Effect Prompt**: Describe the sound effect you want to add.
    - **Lip Sync Prompt**: Enter text for lip sync.
    - **Voice**: Choose a voice for lip sync.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Mastering Prompts for FxCraft

To achieve the best results with FxCraft, focus on descriptive and specific prompts.

### Prompting Techniques:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be Specific, Not Poetic" icon="pencil">
    Use real-world camera lingo (f/1.4, backlight, ISO) and descriptive terms like "frosted glass window" instead of "dreamy vibes."
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Experiment with Aspect Ratios and Styles" icon="palette">
    For Instagram Reels, use 9:16 vertical; for TikTok, try 1:1 square. Mix styles: combine “watercolor” with “futuristic” for a unique aesthetic.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Layer Images for Complex Scenes" icon="images">
    Use the Image-to-Video tool to animate multiple elements separately, then compile them in a video editor.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
