# Lipsync Max: Create Realistic Lip-Synced Videos

<Tip>
  Bring your characters to life with Lipsync Max! Upload an image and an audio file, and watch as the lips are perfectly synced to the sound.
</Tip>

## Overview

Lipsync Max is a powerful tool that allows you to create realistic lip-synced videos with ease. Powered by Bytedance's Omnihuman model, Lipsync Max takes an image and an audio file and generates a video with the lips perfectly synced to the sound.

## How to Use Lipsync Max

Creating your own lip-synced video is a simple two-step process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Upload an Image">
    Upload an image of a person. For best results, make sure the face is centered and unobstructed.
    <img src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/lipsyncmax2.jpeg" alt="Lipsync Max Step 1" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Upload an Audio File">
    Upload an audio file with the sound you want to sync to the video. The audio must be no longer than 30 seconds.
    <audio src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/lipsync2.mp3" controls></audio>
  </Step>
</Steps>
