# LipSync: Create Lip-Synced Videos

<Tip>
  Bring your characters to life with LipSync! Upload a video and an audio file, and watch as the lips are perfectly synced to the sound.
</Tip>

## Overview

LipSync is a powerful tool that allows you to create lip-synced videos with ease. Simply upload a video of a person talking and an audio file, and LipSync will replace the audio and sync the lip movements to the new sound.

## How to Use LipSync

Creating your own lip-synced video is a simple two-step process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Upload a Video">
    Upload a video of a person talking. For best results, make sure the face is clear and visible.
    <video src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/lipsync1.mp4" controls />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Upload an Audio File">
    Upload an audio file with the sound you want to sync to the video.
    <audio src="https://pixiomedia.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/lipsync2.mp3" controls></audio>
  </Step>
</Steps>
