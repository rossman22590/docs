# TalkCraft Pro: Audio-Driven Portrait Animation

<Tip>
  Breathe life into static portraits by syncing them with audio, complete with realistic lip movements and emotional expressions!
</Tip>

## Overview

TalkCraft Pro is an advanced AI tool that takes audio input and a static portrait image to create a dynamic, animated video. This innovative technology syncs lip movements and facial expressions with the audio, bringing still images to life in a remarkably realistic way.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Audio-Driven Animation" icon="waveform">
    Sync lip movements and expressions with audio up to 60 seconds long.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Portrait Animation" icon="user">
    Animate facial features of portraits for a lifelike speaking effect.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Emotional Expression" icon="face-smile">
    Add emotional depth to animations based on audio tone and content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customizable Output" icon="sliders">
    Choose from various aspect ratios to suit your needs.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Use TalkCraft Pro

Follow these steps to create your animated portrait video:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Audio" icon="music">
    Choose an audio file up to 60 seconds long. Preferably of a voice speaking or singing.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://img.mytsi.org/i/tRZA799.png" alt="Audio Selection Interface" />
    </Frame>
    
    <Callout type="warning">
      If your audio is longer than 60 seconds, it will be automatically trimmed to the first 60 seconds.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Portrait Image" icon="image">
    Upload a portrait image where the subject is looking at the camera for best results.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://img.mytsi.org/i/Md1S800.png" alt="Image Upload Interface" />
    </Frame>
    
    <Callout type="info">
      For optimal performance, use images less than 3MB in size. The image should be a portrait with the face taking up the majority of the frame.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Aspect Ratio" icon="crop">
    Choose the desired aspect ratio for your output video.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Character" icon="sparkles">
    Click the "Generate Character" button to start the animation process.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://img.mytsi.org/i/WXEe801.png" alt="Generation Progress" />
    </Frame>
    
    <Callout type="warning">
      This feature uses credits. 1 second of audio = 1 credit.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices for TalkCraft Pro

To get the most out of TalkCraft Pro, consider these tips:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Choosing the Right Audio" icon="volume-high">
    - Use clear, high-quality audio recordings
    - Ensure the voice is prominent and easily distinguishable
    - Consider using audio with varying emotional tones for more dynamic animations
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Selecting the Perfect Portrait" icon="camera">
    - Choose a high-resolution portrait with the face clearly visible
    - Ensure the subject is looking directly at the camera
    - Use images with good lighting and minimal background distractions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Optimizing for Different Aspect Ratios" icon="tv">
    - Consider your intended platform (e.g., Instagram, YouTube, TikTok) when selecting aspect ratio
    - Ensure your portrait image has enough surrounding space to accommodate different aspect ratios
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Managing Credit Usage" icon="coins">
    - Plan your audio length carefully to optimize credit usage
    - Consider trimming longer audio files to focus on the most important parts
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with TalkCraft Pro, try these solutions:

1. **Poor Lip-Sync**: Ensure your audio is clear and the portrait has a well-defined mouth area.
2. **Unnatural Expressions**: Try using audio with more emotional variety to improve expression mapping.
3. **Distorted Animation**: Make sure your portrait image is high-quality and the face is the main focus.
4. **Generation Fails**: Check that your audio and image files meet the size and format requirements.
